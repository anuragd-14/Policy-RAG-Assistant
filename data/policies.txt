**Refund Policy**
We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. You may return eligible items within 30 days of the delivery date for a full refund. To be eligible for a return, your item must be unused, in the same condition that you received it, and in the original packaging with all tags attached. Refunds will be processed to the original method of payment within 7-10 business days after we receive and inspect your return. Please note that "Final Sale" items, gift cards, and perishable goods cannot be returned.

**Shipping Policy**
We currently ship to the United States, Canada, and the United Kingdom. Standard shipping is a flat rate of $5.99 for all orders under $50. Orders of $50 or more qualify for free Standard Shipping. Standard delivery typically takes 3-5 business days. Express Shipping (1-2 business days) is available for a flat rate of $14.99. Please note that we do not ship to P.O. boxes or military (APO/FPO) addresses at this time.

**Cancellation Policy**
Orders can be cancelled free of charge within 12 hours of placement. If you wish to cancel after 12 hours, the order may have already been processed or shipped. In such cases, you will need to wait for the item to arrive and then follow our Return Policy. To initiate a cancellation, please email support@fakestore.com immediately.
